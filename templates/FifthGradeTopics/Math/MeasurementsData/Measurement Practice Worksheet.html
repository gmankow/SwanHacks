  1. 36 inches = ___ feet

Part B: Metric Measurement Conversions

Convert using powers of ten.

  1. 600 centimeters = ___ meters
  2. 5 liters = ___ milliliters
  3. 3.4 kilometers = ___ meters
  4. 450 grams = ___ kilograms
  5. 7,200 milliliters = ___ liters

  1. 2 feet ___ 30 inches
  2. 1.5 liters ___ 1,200 mL
