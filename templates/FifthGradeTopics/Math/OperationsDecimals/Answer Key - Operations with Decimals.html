Part I: Challenge Questions

  • Multiplying by 0.1 shifts each digit one place to the right because each place becomes 1/10 of its value.
    Example: 4.5 → 0.45
  • Dividing by 0.01 makes the number 100 times larger because 0.01 is one hundredth.
    4.5 ÷ 0.01 = 450
    (You’re finding how many hundredths fit into 4.5.)
